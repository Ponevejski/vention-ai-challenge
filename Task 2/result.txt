### **Bug Report**  

#### **Title**  
**Logout Button Unresponsive in Safari Browser**  

#### **Description**  
The logout button fails to trigger any action when clicked in Safari, preventing users from signing out.  

#### **Steps to Reproduce**  
1. Log in to the application.  
2. Navigate to any page containing the logout button.  
3. Click the logout button.  

#### **Expected vs. Actual Behavior**  
| **Expected** | **Actual** |  
|--------------|------------|  
| Clicking logout should sign the user out and redirect to the login page. | No response occurs; the button appears unclickable or ignores input. |  

#### **Environment**  
- **OS:** macOS Ventura  
- **Browser:** Safari 16.3  
- **Device:** MacBook Pro (M1)
